Who are we ?

Resonance Racing is a student run organization of AISSMS College of Engineering, in which we design, manufacture, and test an All Terrain Vehicle to participate in the BAJA SAE competition.By emphasizing rigorous engineering, technical expertise and effective management, Resonance Racing empowers students to become engineering leaders. We are a highly motivated and close knit group of 31 members with a strong alumni network. The motive behind this organization is to foster the growth of students, pushing ourselves beyond our limits and become able professionals. We promote collaborative and creative efforts, which helps us learn and develop problem solving skills using creative methods. Besides these, students learn skills like project planning, management, marketing, finance, amongst many. All of this is made possible by our engineering college and supported by our gracious sponsors who enable us to reach creative and technical heights every year

What is BAJA SAE ?

BAJA SAE is a student competition organised by the Society of Automotive Engineers, in which over a hundred teams participate every year and compete in various dynamic events like acceleration, maneuverability, rock crowl, and more which test the vehicle performance, an endurance race which tests the durability of the vehicle over difficult terrains. Teams are also judged on the basis of their design and design process, as well as events like CAE, costing and sales. Having a strong background and history, we are currently in our 13th season, and in the process of building an All wheel drive vehicle for the first time in our history.
